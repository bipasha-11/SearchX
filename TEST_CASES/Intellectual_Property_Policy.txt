INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY POLICY - CORPORATE
--------------------------------------
This Policy outlines the ownership and protection of Intellectual Property (IP) at LEGAL_TECH_CORP.
1. OWNERSHIP: Any IP created during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Company.
2. PROTECTION: Employees must document all inventions and innovations in the Company's repository.
3. INVENTIONS: All rights to patents, trademarks, and copyright are assigned to the Company.
4. BREACH: Disclosure of trade secrets will result in immediate termination and legal action.
5. GOVERNANCE: Governed by the Intellectual Property Rights Act of the country.
