CASE SUMMARY: STATE VS. JOHN DOE
--------------------------------
COURT: High Court, Delhi (Criminal Side)
CASE NO: CRL.REV.102/2023
HEARING DATE: Dec 15, 2023
FACTS: The accused, John Doe, was charged with fraud and misappropriation of funds.
ISSUE: Whether the intent to defraud was proven beyond reasonable doubt.
RULING: The court found that the evidence was circumstantial and the intent was not established.
JUDGMENT: Accused is acquitted on all charges. Order of the lower court is set aside.
