SUPREME COURT JUDGMENT: RE_2023
-------------------------------
CASE: Union of India vs. ABC Corp
CITATION: 2023 SCC 456
JUDGMENT DATE: October 10, 2023
BENCH: Justice S. Kumar, Justice R. Patel
ISSUE: Whether the arbitration agreement was valid in the absence of signature.
RULING: The Court held that a signature is not mandatory if the intent to arbitrate is clear.
JUDGMENT: Appeal dismissed. Arbitration proceedings affirmed.
