ARBITRATION CLAUSE - DISPUTE RESOLUTION
--------------------------------------
This Arbitration Clause ("Clause") is intended to be incorporated into all standard legal contracts.
1. ARBITRATION: All disputes shall be settled through binding arbitration.
2. SELECTION: The arbitrator shall be chosen from the Panel of Neutral Arbitrators.
3. JURISDICTION: This Clause is governed by the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.
4. VENUE: The venue for arbitration shall be Bangalore, Karnataka.
