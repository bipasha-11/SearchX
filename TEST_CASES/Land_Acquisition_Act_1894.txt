LAND ACQUISITION ACT, 1894
--------------------------
SECTION 4: Publication of Preliminary Notification
1. Whenever it appears to the Government that land is needed for a public purpose.
2. The Collector shall cause public notice of the substance of such notification.
3. Every notification under Section 4 shall be published in the Official Gazette.
4. This Act provides for the acquisition of land for companies and public works.
