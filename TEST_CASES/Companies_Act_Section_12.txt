COMPANIES ACT, 2013 - SECTION 12
--------------------------------
SECTION 12: Registered Office of Company
1. A company shall, on and from the fifteenth day of its incorporation, have a registered office.
2. The registered office shall be capable of receiving and acknowledging all communications.
3. Every company shall paint or affix its name and the address of its registered office.
4. Violation of Section 12 results in a fine for every day during which the default continues.
