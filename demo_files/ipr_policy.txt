The Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Policy of the Organization aims to protect the creative and innovative works of its employees.
It covers Patents, Trademarks, Copyrights, and Trade Secrets.
All inventions made during the course of employment shall be the sole property of the Organization.
Employees are required to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) to prevent unauthorized use of sensitive data.
Any infringement of these rights will lead to strict legal action under the relevant IP laws.
